# flockdocs

Primary-source documents related to FlockBlocker's research subjects.

## Contents

This directory holds documents obtained directly from municipal entities (via FOIA, public records, or correspondence) and from Flock Safety, including:

- **City correspondence** — letters, contracts, council communications, and FOIA responses produced by city governments contracting with Flock Safety.
- **Flock Safety legal correspondence** — including the cease and desist letter issued to this project.

## Provenance

Each document should be accompanied by provenance metadata where possible:

- Source agency or party
- Date received
- Method of acquisition (FOIA request ID, direct mail, email, etc.)
- SHA-256 hash for integrity verification

## Use

These are primary sources. They are preserved here unaltered. Analysis, redactions, or annotated versions belong in adjacent files clearly marked as derivative.
