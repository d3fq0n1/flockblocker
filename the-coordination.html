What This Page Documents

On April 16, 2026, Flock Safety's Chief Legal Officer served a cease-and-desist letter on the operator of this site. The letter demanded takedown of flockblocker.org within seven calendar days, citing defamation per se under Wisconsin law.

On April 23, 2026, the City of Mauston produced records in response to the operator's request under Wis. Stat. § 19.35. The records included communications between Flock Safety personnel and City of Mauston personnel, dated before, during, and after the cease-and-desist period. The records were produced by the City Administrator and were not obtained through any other channel.

This page documents those communications in chronological order. Each entry below cites its source: the city records production, the date of release, and the original email PDF as produced. Additional entries will be added as further records are released.
