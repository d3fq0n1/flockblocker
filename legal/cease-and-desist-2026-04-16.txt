PARAGRAPH 1
I write concerning your unauthorized and unlawful creation,
ownership, and operation of the website "flockblocker.org"
(the "Website"). In addition to numerous false, misleading,
and/or defamatory representations about Flock Safety and its
technologies, operations, and personnel, this Website
presently contains demonstrably false, misleading, and
defamatory statements regarding a specific Flock employee at
https://flockblocker.org/wall-of-shame.html.


PARAGRAPH 2
Flock hereby demands that you immediately cease and desist
all unlawful activities described herein, specifically:


PARAGRAPH 3 (numbered demand 1)
1. IMMEDIATELY taking down, de-indexing, and permanently
   deleting all defamatory and false content on the Website
   "flockblocker.org," and on all associated social media
   accounts, mirror sites, and archived versions.


PARAGRAPH 4 (numbered demand 2)
2. IMMEDIATELY removing all false, misleading, and defamatory
   content concerning the Flock employee Randy Gluck, and any
   other Flock personnel or the company itself, from all
   online platforms, including but not limited to any
   remaining websites, social media, forums, and discussion
   boards.


PARAGRAPH 5 (numbered demand 3)
3. IMMEDIATELY providing written confirmation within seven
   (7) calendar days of the date of this Notice that you have
   complied with all of the demands set forth herein.


PARAGRAPH 6
The content published on "flockblocker.org" contains
statements of fact concerning the Flock employee that are
patently false and untrue. Under Wisconsin law and common law
principles, these statements are defamatory per se, causing
irreparable harm to the employee's professional and personal
reputation, and resulting in ongoing damages to Mr. Gluck and
to Flock.


PARAGRAPH 7
Defamation consists of a statement that is published to a
third party, is false, and tends to harm the reputation of
the individual (See Torgerson v. Journal/Sentinel, Inc., 210
Wis. 2d 525 (1997)). Given that the inflammatory and false
statements made on your Website pertain directly to the
employee's professional and personal conduct and integrity,
they constitute defamation per se, meaning falsity and
damages are presumed.


PARAGRAPH 8
Your publication of these statements demonstrates a malicious
intent or, at minimum, a reckless disregard for the truth,
and is not excused or validated by your citation to equally
false assertions by others.


PARAGRAPH 9
Your failure to immediately comply with the demands set forth
above will leave Flock with no alternative but to pursue all
available legal remedies against you, including, but not
limited to:


PARAGRAPH 10 (numbered remedy 1)
1. Filing a lawsuit for defamation and related torts in the
   appropriate federal or Wisconsin state court.


PARAGRAPH 11 (numbered remedy 2)
2. Seeking a temporary restraining order and permanent
   injunction to compel the immediate removal of the content
   and the shutdown of the Website, as provided under Rule 65
   of the Federal Rules of Civil Procedure or corresponding
   Wisconsin Rules.


PARAGRAPH 12 (numbered remedy 3)
3. Seeking monetary damages, which include actual damages,
   punitive damages for malicious conduct, and recovery of
   all attorneys' fees and costs incurred in enforcing
   Flock's rights.


PARAGRAPH 13
This letter is not an exhaustive statement of Flock's rights
and remedies, all of which are expressly reserved.


PARAGRAPH 14
We trust that you will give this matter the immediate
attention it requires and comply fully with these demands to
avoid further legal action.
