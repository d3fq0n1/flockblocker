# /optical

Retroreflective and IR-based interference approaches.

## Scope

- Retroreflective material properties and their interaction with IR illuminators
- Near-infrared (NIR) LED arrays and their effect on CMOS sensors at LPR wavelengths
- Passive optical approaches: coatings, films, and material science
- Analysis of Flock camera hardware (IR flash characteristics, sensor sensitivity curves)
- Distinction between read-denial (plate unreadable) and read-corruption (plate misread)

## Note

Read-denial is the wrong goal. A failed capture is discarded and has zero downstream impact.
The interesting question is whether optical methods can induce *consistent, confident misreads*
rather than simply degrading image quality.
